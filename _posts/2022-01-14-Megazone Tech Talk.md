After giving a poster presentation at [2021 AI retreat](https://kmario4180.github.io/2021/11/14/Poster-presentation-at-2021-AI-retreat.html), I was invited to give a talk at [Megazone](https://www.megazone.com/us/) Tech Talk!  
<br/>
I gave a talk on my master theis, titled "Identifying unique neurocognitive mechanisms of food addiction and binge eating using longitudinal study design and computational modeling." 
<br/>
It was a great opportunity to introduce my research and also learn about Megazone. They not only provide cloud services but also offer AI-based consultations to various industries, such as wine recommendation systems or analyzing the impact of Blackpink on YG Entertainment's building by aggregating data from various social media platforms worldwide. It was quite fascinating!
