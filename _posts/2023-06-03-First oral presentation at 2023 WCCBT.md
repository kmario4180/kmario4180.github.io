I gave a oral presentation at [2023 WCCBT](https://wccbt2023.org/), titled "Shared and distinct neurocognitive features of internet gaming disorder and alcohol use disorder" <br/>

By collaborating with psychiatric department in Yonsei university, we analyzed task fMRI data from individuals with internet gaming disorder, alcohol use disorder, and healthy control while they perfomred the two-stage Markov decision task. 
<br/>

It was a significant milestone for me as it was my first time presenting at an international conference. I found the experience to be highly rewarding, as I had the change to showcase my research, engage in discussions about my other projects, and interact with researchers from different locations! I am excited to share that we are in the final stages of completing our work and preparing it for submission!
