I had orignially planned to attend the meeting in person, but it was canceled due to the COVID-19. <br/>
However, I still enjoyed engaging in discussions with our group members and sharing our researche and motivaions through virtual poster session! :)  
If you have registered for the 2021 SfN, please check out the poster session! You can view my poster and also 5-minute recordings (_Day two, P778 - Modeling and Behavior III #03_) <br/>
