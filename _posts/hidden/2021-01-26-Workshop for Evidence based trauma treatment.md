I took the workshop for Evidence-based trauma treament held by Dr. Hye Sun Joo in [Korea Trauma Research & Education Institute](http://www.ktlink.co.kr/sub06/sub01.php). <br/>
The lecture first addressed real examples of PTSD patients, and then discussed how to respond in certain situations with specific exampels. <br/>
Since I've never experienced PTSD patients before, this workshop will be helpful for me to work as a counselor at the on-campus counseling center.
