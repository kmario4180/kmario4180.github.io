Starting this January, I have begun working at the on-campus counseling center at Seoul National University, known as the ["Center for Campus Life & Culture"](https://snucounsel.snu.ac.kr/en/main.do). <br/>
As a graduate student majoring in clinical psychology, this is a fantastic opportunity for me to gain experience in real clinical settings and apply what I have learned in lectures so far. <br/>
From January to June, I will be conducting intake interviews on a weekly basis. Additionally, I will be conducting 1:1 counseling sessions! <br/>

As a practice counselor, I am eager to learn and grow, and I hope to provide valuable assistance to my future clients!
