I am a graduate student in the [CCS Lab](https://ccs-lab.github.io/) (September 2019 - Present). I received a Bachelor’s degree in Psychology from Seoul National University with summa cum laude in August 2019, and received a Master's degree in Clinical Psychology from Seoul National University in August 2021. I am broadly interested in addictions and decision-making. Using neuroscientific and computational approaches, I hope to identify markers of addiction-related behaviors that have clinical utility, and to develop predictive models for real-life decision-making (e.g. consumer's decision-making, irrational food consumption in obese individuals).
