I am a PhD student in the [CCS Lab](https://ccs-lab.github.io/) (September 2021 - Present). I received a Bachelor’s degree in Psychology from Seoul National University with summa cum laude in August 2019, and received a Master's degree in Clinical Psychology from Seoul National University in August 2021. 

My research interests revolve around the fields of addictions and decision-making. By leveraging neuroscientific and computational approaches, I aim to discover meaningful markers that are indicative of addiction-related behaviors with practical clinical applications. Additionally, I aspire to construct predictive models that can effectively anticipate real-life decision-making scenarios, such as craving and relapse in addiction.
