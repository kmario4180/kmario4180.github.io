I gave a presentation on "Classifying Autism Spectrum Disorder Using the Temporal Statistics of Resting-State Functional MRI Data With 3D Convolutional Neural Networks" by Thomas et al. (2020). The paper tried classifying Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) from healthy control using resting-state fMRI, with 3D Convolutional Neural Network (3D-CNN). Unlike the conventional way of spatial downscaling of fMRI data using brain atlas, the authors tried to reduce the dimensionality of time-domain using multiple summary measures of connectivity! Even though the highest prediction accuracy achieved from multi-measure ensemble approach was not high enough compared to other studies, it was a new perspective of dealing with fMRI data to me!
