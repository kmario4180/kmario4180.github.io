I gave a presentation on the paper "Neural basis of video gaming: a systematic review" by Palaus et al. (2017). Addiction is an attractive topic, in the respect of irrational decision making. So I decided to present this paper related to the "gaming disorder", a topic with an active discussion about whether we should define it as a mental disorder. Together with other two papers discussed in the class, we could think & discuss about the issue of gaming disorder.
