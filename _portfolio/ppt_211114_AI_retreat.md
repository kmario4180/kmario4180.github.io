I gave a talk at "Seminar on Psychology" course in Seoul National University. 

The title was "Identifying predictors and mechanisms of maladaptive decision-making". It was my first time giving a talk introducing my research to undergraduates and other graduate students. I shared two of my studies: my first-first author paper, Daily caffeinated soda intake as a warning sign indicating later alcohol initiation, and my master thesis, Identifying shaerd and unique mechanisms between food addiction and binge eating based on body image concern.
