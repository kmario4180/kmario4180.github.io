I gave a presentation on the paper "Blunted ventral striatal responses to anticipated reward foreshadow problematic drug use in novelty-seeking adolescents" by Buchel et al. (2017). I believe predicting future drug use with longitudinal data could help identify the mechanism of substance use/misuses such as risk factors, and further lead to effective early interventions. Since I'm working with other large-sample longitudinal data (<a href="https://nda.nih.gov/abcd"> ABCD project </a>), and looking forward to do such prediction, this paper was a great reference. Also, I hosted a class discussion in the *seminar in psychopathology*, and we mainly talked about "socioeconomic factors that we should consider when investigating substance use" and "effective intervention approach considering possible side effects".
